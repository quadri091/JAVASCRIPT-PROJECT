Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all major locations within the city and nearby surrounding areas. You just have to choose any location suitable for you!.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3 - 5 minutes no matter how far you are. In most cases, orders placed early in the day are delivered in the next 4 minutes.

Are the appliances safe to use?

Yes. Our devices goes through strict testing and quality-control processes to ensure it is easy-handling, safe, and comfortable for living.

What payments method do you accept?

We accept secure online payments via Paystack, which supports debit cards, bank transfers, USSD, and other safe payment options.

How do i return damaged devices?

Simply hand over your damaged devices to our delivery agent during your next delivery or schedule a pickup if you don’t have an upcoming order.

What if i'm not home during delivery?

If you're not available, our delivery agent will contact you to if we are to stop the delivery or arrange a safe drop-off location.
